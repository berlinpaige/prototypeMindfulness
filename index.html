Don't like coffee? Here is a recipe for tea instead!


Smooth Sweet Tea

Prep: 5min. Cook: 15min. Ready in: 3hr 20min.

Ingredients:

1 pinch baking soda

2 cups boiling water

6 tea bags

6 cups cool water

3/4 cup white sugar

Directions: Sprinkle a pinch of baking soda into a 64-ounce, heat-proof, glass pitcher. Pour in boiling water, and add tea bags. Cover, and allow to steep for 15 minutes. Watch Now Remove tea bags, and discard; stir in sugar until dissolved. Pour in cool water, then refrigerate until cold

You discovered the Earth Club!

Earth Action is a student-run club on the St. Scholastica campus aiming to promote living sustainably and being good stewards of the Earth. Our focus is not solely on the college campus, but also on the surrounding community and the Earth as a whole. We aim to instill in students values that they will carry with them through college and their adult lives, preparing them for responsible living and meaningful work.
